📈 Stock Tickers

Enter the symbols you want to track on the dashboard, separated by commas. Supports stocks (TSLA, SPY, AAPL), S&P 500 (SPX), and crypto (BTC, ETH, SOL).

e.g. BTC, ETH, TSLA, AAPL, SPX, SPY

📷 Cameras

Add your camera streams. Streams are proxied through go2rtc (running on port 1984) so they can be viewed in your browser. Supports RTSP, RTSPS, and other go2rtc-compatible sources.

Camera name must be unique and contain no spaces. The RTSP URL is kept server-side and never sent to the browser.
